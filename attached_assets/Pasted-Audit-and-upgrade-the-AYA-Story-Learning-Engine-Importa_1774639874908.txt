Do NOT create a new system before verifying whether a story-based learning system already exists in the codebase.

The goal is to allow AYA to teach concepts through interactive stories where the student participates in the narrative.

PHASE 3 — STORY LEARNING ENGINE

If the system is missing or incomplete, implement the AYA Story Learning Engine.

AYA should teach through interactive story scenarios.

Example structure:

Story intro  
Problem  
Student decision  
Learning moment  
Challenge  
Story continuation

------------------------------------------------

STORY STRUCTURE

Each story lesson should follow this format:

1. Introduction

AYA begins a short story.

Example:

"Маги и нейната котка Мая откриха 6 ябълки в градината."

2. Situation

AYA presents a situation related to the lesson.

Example:

"Ако Маги иска да раздели ябълките между 2 приятели..."

3. Question

AYA asks the student what should happen next.

Example:

"Колко ябълки ще получи всеки?"

4. Student answer

Student responds via:

typing  
choice selection  
voice (future support)

5. Story continuation

AYA continues the story depending on the answer.

Correct answer:

"Точно така! Всеки получава по 3 ябълки."

Incorrect answer:

"Почти! Нека помислим още веднъж."

------------------------------------------------

INTERACTIVE ELEMENTS

Stories should include:

• decision points  
• mini puzzles  
• math challenges  
• vocabulary questions  
• logical reasoning  

The student should feel part of the story.

------------------------------------------------

AVATAR ROLE

AYA avatar becomes the narrator.

Avatar animations:

• explaining  
• thinking  
• celebrating success  
• encouraging retry  

------------------------------------------------

STORY TYPES

Stories may include:

Math adventures  
Science discovery stories  
Language puzzles  
History journeys  
Nature exploration  

------------------------------------------------

ADAPTIVE STORY INTEGRATION

Integrate with Adaptive Learning Brain.

If a student struggles with fractions:

AYA can start a story involving sharing food or objects.

If a student struggles with reading:

AYA can offer shorter story segments.

------------------------------------------------

CURIOSITY ENGINE CONNECTION

Stories should also trigger curiosity.

Example:

"Знаеш ли защо това работи така?"

This should lead to optional exploration.

------------------------------------------------

• Story lesson

Story adventure  
Practice problems  
Mini test

Use existing lesson/chat architecture where possible.
