AYA CURIOSITY ENGINE FEATURES

The Curiosity Engine should make students WANT to continue learning.

AYA should naturally encourage exploration through:

1. Curiosity prompts

After a lesson AYA might say:

"Искаш ли да разберем нещо още по-интересно?"

or

"Знаеш ли защо това работи така?"

These prompts should invite discovery.

--------------------------------

2. Exploration cards

AYA suggests optional learning paths like:

• “Интересен факт”
• “Мини предизвикателство”
• “Да проверим дали знаеш това”
• “Малка загадка”

These should feel playful, not mandatory.

--------------------------------

3. Discovery questions

AYA occasionally asks surprising questions like:

"Знаеш ли защо планетите не падат?"

or

"Как мислиш, защо някои животни могат да виждат в тъмното?"

These questions should spark curiosity.

--------------------------------

4. Learning streak encouragement

When the student returns multiple days:

AYA acknowledges the streak:

"Трети ден учим заедно! Готов ли си за нещо ново?"

--------------------------------

5. Adaptive curiosity

Use Adaptive Learning Brain data to trigger curiosity prompts when:

• student is bored (too many correct answers quickly)
• student is stuck
• student finishes a lesson early

--------------------------------

6. Fun mini exploration

Examples:

• mini riddles
• quick facts
• tiny experiments
• “Did you know?” moments

--------------------------------

• simple curiosity triggers
