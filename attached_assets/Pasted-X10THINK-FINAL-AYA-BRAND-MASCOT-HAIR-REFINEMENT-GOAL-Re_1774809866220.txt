X10THINK — FINAL AYA BRAND MASCOT HAIR REFINEMENT

GOAL  
Refine the AYA avatar hair so the character becomes a stronger and more natural brand mascot suitable for an educational app.

IMPORTANT RULES  
Modify ONLY the hair and hairline.  
Do NOT change:
- face shape  
- glasses  
- eyes  
- skin tone  
- body proportions  
- character colors  

Keep the same visual identity.

------------------------------------------------

FOREHEAD ADJUSTMENT

The forehead currently appears too large.

Reduce the visible forehead slightly.

Hair should start lower and gently overlap the top of the forehead.

Hair must look like it naturally grows from the scalp, not floating above the head.

------------------------------------------------

FRINGE / HAIR FALL

Add a very soft natural front fall.

Hair should slightly fall toward the forehead, similar to natural children's hair.

This fall must be subtle:
- not bangs
- not covering the eyes
- only soft framing of the forehead.

------------------------------------------------

TOP HAIR SHAPE

The top of the hair should form a soft rounded dome.

Avoid flat top shapes.

Hair must follow the skull curve naturally.

------------------------------------------------

SIDE STRANDS

Side strands should:

• frame the face naturally  
• slightly curve inward near the cheeks  
• then curve slightly outward near the bottom  

Avoid perfectly straight vertical lines.

------------------------------------------------

COLOR

Hair color must remain a single dark chestnut brown.

Remove any black tones or dual color shading.

Only one consistent hair color.

------------------------------------------------

BRAND SILHOUETTE

The hair silhouette must stay simple and recognizable.

AYA should remain identifiable even at small icon sizes (24px–64px).

------------------------------------------------

REALISM GOAL

Hair should look:

• natural  
• soft  
• child-friendly  
• integrated with the head  

Avoid the "wig effect".

Hair must sit directly on the scalp with a natural hairline.

------------------------------------------------

FINAL CHECK

Confirm:

• smaller visible forehead  
• soft hair falling slightly toward the forehead  
• hair attached naturally to the head  
• face and glasses unchanged  
• consistent hair color
