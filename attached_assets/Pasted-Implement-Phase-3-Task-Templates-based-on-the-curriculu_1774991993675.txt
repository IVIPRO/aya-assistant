--------------------------------
TASK TEMPLATE TYPES
--------------------------------

Create reusable templates for math topics.

1️⃣ Solved Example

Structure:

problem
step-by-step explanation
final answer
short reasoning

Example:

35 + 6

Разделяме 35 на десетици и единици.

35 = 3 дес. и 5 ед.

Събираме единиците:
5 + 6 = 11

Пишем 1 и пренасяме 1 десетица.

Сега десетиците:
3 + 1 = 4

Отговор:
41

--------------------------------

2️⃣ Guided Practice

Structure:

problem

retry option

--------------------------------

3️⃣ Independent Practice

Structure:

problem
student answer input
answer validation

retry

--------------------------------

4️⃣ Short Test Task

Structure:

problem

WORD PROBLEMS

Add support for:

story problem
operation detection
reasoning explanation
result

Example:

34 стикера се разделят поравно между 26 деца.

34 ÷ 26

Всяко дете получава 1 стикер.

Остават 8.

--------------------------------

RULES

1️⃣ Templates must reuse the curriculum structure.

Use:

getTopicsForGradeAndSubject()

2️⃣ Difficulty must match grade.

Example:

Grade 2:
- numbers up to 100
- no decimals
- simple word problems

Grade 5:
- decimals
- larger numbers
- more complex word problems

3️⃣ Do not generate thousands of tasks.

Only create the template engine.
